Week 2 - Design Principles

Jesse Earley

Proximity

Amazon

https://www.amazon.com/

Within the amazon website, we see many different products next to their descriptions. It also shows differering sections next to related content. It also provides different categories and sub categories that are within close proximity of one another showing relationships.

White Space

Walmart

https://www.walmart.com/

In the Walmart website, there is ample white space around every title, description and anywhere you type. This allows for a clear reading that does not cause any eye strain or confusion of the website.

Contrast

EPS Energy

https://epsenergy.com/

Electric Power Systems website has contrasting colors that keep things very simple. With the black background, their logos and wording are in either the colors, yellow or white. It provides great contrast that makes every part of the website readable.
