Target Audience:

This website is to help the business and residential growth within Logan Utah. It is designed to give the people resources and information so that they may be able to help make a change in Logan for the better.

Personas:

Thomas: Thomas is a graduate from Utah State University in Business Management. He wants to create his own phone repair shop but needs to know the logistics concerning taxes and finding a place to set up shop. He would like to start small but eventually expand into other cities.

Genia: Genia just started a nutrition company and needs to make sure that her company is up to code with her product. She also wants to be able to connect with gyms around the area to try and promote her famous protein packed health shakes.

Scenarios

  1. An entreprenuer from another city wants to expand into the Logan area. He came to recieve information and prospects for his business here.
  2. Thomas needs to know how to make his business official and know what documents needs to be filled out.
  3. A restaurant owner is strugging with the inflation of grocery prices and wants to connect with others to know if they have the same problem.
  4. Genia has not had any luck with selling her product at gyms and wants to explore who else might be intersted.
  5. A company wants to look into creating batteries for airplains and other large vehicles. They want more info on the airport in Logan and to know what kind of competition there may be.
